Adastra manifesto

Humanity is entering a new stage of transformation and global adaptation, giving way to a new technological era in which its humanization will be key to coexisting in the new reality in which more and more people bet based on the blockchain with a decentralized and equitable model that allows digital equality in opportunities and growth.- Our approach is fundamentally based on two principles:

‚Äç1) Support the launch of projects based on any blockchain platform that apply and improve the real world.

2) Bring the world of blockchain closer to investors from the traditional economy, making new investment possibilities available to them in a way that is as clear and simple as possible.

We want to eliminate any type of friction between economic and investment concepts of the blockchain, thus allowing the approach of new and future participants in the decentralized ecosystem and the new world economy. Adastra is conceived under the premise of being a fair, equitable, and qualitative Launchpad, based mainly on the Cardano environment due to its great scalability, security, and projection, in the same way, we want to serve and support unique projects of any kind based on this technology. Our unique and equitable participation system is designed to attract great ideas, delgatorswho prefer to bet on projects based on proof-of-life and single pools that guarantee equality and decentralization.

Our vision is simple, never stop growing and adapting, constantly implementing new features that allow us to broaden horizons and ensure our scalability in a unique but changing and mouldable environment, so as never to be left behind.

Our Mission Statement

  • To provide a fair and equitable launchpad for participants and projects alike.
  • To support the growth of Cardano by empowering single-pool operators.
  • To facilitate the user experience and access to new and innovative ways of investing in decentralized and human-centered projects.
